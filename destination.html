Explore Wayanad

Discover the enchanting beauty, history, and serenity of Wayanad through its remarkable attractions.

Edakkal Caves

Step into history with the ancient Edakkal Caves, adorned with Neolithic carvings that tell stories of a bygone era.

Chembra Peak

Renowned for its heart-shaped lake and breathtaking views, Chembra Peak is a trekker's paradise.

Banasura Sagar Dam

The largest earthen dam in India, Banasura Sagar offers a serene escape with stunning views and boating experiences.

Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary

Explore the rich biodiversity of Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary, home to elephants, tigers, and exotic flora and fauna.

Meenmutty Waterfalls

Witness the majestic three-tiered Meenmutty Waterfalls, cascading through lush greenery and offering a tranquil retreat.

Pookode Lake

A serene freshwater lake nestled amidst lush forests, Pookode Lake offers scenic boating experiences and relaxing vibes.

Soochipara Waterfalls

Surrounded by dense forests, the three-tiered Soochipara Waterfalls is a perfect spot for adventure and tranquility.

Thirunelli Temple

Nestled in the Brahmagiri hills, this ancient temple is a spiritual haven with its serene and sacred ambiance.

Kuruva Island

An enchanting group of islets on the Kabini River, Kuruva Island is a nature lover's paradise.

Lakkidi View Point

Known as the 'Gateway of Wayanad', Lakkidi offers breathtaking views of the lush green valleys and winding roads.

Wayanad Heritage Museum

Explore the rich history and culture of Wayanad through ancient artifacts and exhibits.

Neelimala Viewpoint

An idyllic viewpoint offering a stunning view of lush green valleys and cascading waterfalls.

Phantom Rock

A natural rock formation resembling a human skull, making it a unique attraction in Wayanad.

Pakshipathalam Bird Sanctuary

A paradise for bird watchers, with trekking trails and scenic surroundings.

Kanthanpara Waterfalls

A picturesque waterfall surrounded by lush greenery, perfect for a day trip.

Karlad Lake

A beautiful lake surrounded by lush greenery, ideal for boat rides and nature walks.

900 Kandi Trek

A challenging and rewarding trek offering panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.

Karapuzha Dam

A tranquil dam surrounded by lush green hills, perfect for a peaceful retreat and photography.

Kurumbalakotta

A serene hilltop destination offering panoramic views and a perfect spot for trekking and nature walks.
